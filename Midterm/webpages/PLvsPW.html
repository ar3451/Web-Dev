Professor Layton vs. Phoenix Wright: Ace Attorney

Professor Layton vs. Phoenix Wright: Ace Attorney is a visual novel adventure puzzle video game developed by Level-5 and Capcom and published by Nintendo (Level-5 in Japan) for the Nintendo 3DS. It is a crossover between Level-5's Professor Layton puzzle series and Capcom's Ace Attorney adventure series, combining gameplay elements from both series. The game features scenario designs from Shu Takumi, the series director for the Ace Attorney series. The game was released in Japan on November 29, 2012, March 28, 2014 in Europe, March 29, 2014 in Australia, and August 29, 2014 in North America.
